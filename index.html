Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a very green country with lots of natural attractions and wildlife. Its map happens to surround an Archean craton. In geology, this means a particularly old and thick piece of continental crust which is usually rich in minerals. The country has an abundance of most known minerals which are underexplored due to less developed mining technology. This is a particularly interesting fact to me as I have a background in geology.
