Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a very green country with lots of natural attractions and wildlife. Its map happens to surround an achean craton. In geology this means a particularly old and thick piece of continental crust which is usually rich in minerals. The country has an abundance of most known minerals which are under explored due to less developed mining technology. This is a particularly interesting fact to me as I have a background in geology.
